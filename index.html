WordleRacer

The rules for playing Wordle are as follows:

The goal is to guess a 5-letter word in 6 attempts or less.

After each guess, the letters will be colored to give you feedback:

• Green: The letter is in the correct position in the answer.

• Yellow: The letter is in the answer, but not in the correct position.

• Gray: The letter is not in the answer at all.

Use the colored feedback to inform your next guess.

The game ends when you correctly guess the 5-letter word or use up all 6 attempts.

That's it! Simple, yet challenging and fun.
